About Us

A space for play, culture, and community

Juegoteca Sin Fin is a non-profit organization that, since 1998, has dedicated itself to promoting play, leisure, and cultural activities for children, youth, and adults as a fundamental human right.

Our goal is to contribute to people's holistic development and foster social integration through play and community cultural initiatives.

1998

Since our founding, we have focused on a fundamental mission: social development through play and recreation for children and adults in our neighborhood.

What We Do

Workshops and activities for the entire community

Our activities promote social inclusion, active participation, and personal development through creative learning, play, and open access to cultural resources.

🎲

Free Play

Organized, competitive, and cooperative games for multiple ages. For boys and girls.

🎨

Visual Arts

Experimentation with conventional and non-conventional artistic techniques. For children aged 6 to 12.

📖

Storytelling

Exploring the world of books through reading aloud, games, theatrical performances, and drawing. For boys and girls.

🧠

Memory Workshop

Fun exercises to stimulate memory and enjoy a welcoming, shared space with peers. For older adults.

🎵

Canto

Group singing featuring a repertoire chosen by the participants themselves. For adults.

✍️

Creative Writing

Sharing our own life stories or inventing brand-new ones. No prior experience needed. For adults.

📚

Mafalda Library

More than 2,000 books for all ages, available for loan to our local community.

🤝

Networking

We actively participate in the Boulogne local organization network, working as a team to improve our neighborhood.

🎓

University Internships

We host students from various universities who complete their community service internships at our organization.

Where Juegoteca was born in 1998. The historical heart of our organization.

🏘️ Bajo Boulogne

One of the key communities we reach through our activities and workshops.

🏘️ San Isidro Neighborhood

Where our current headquarters is located, at Escalera 86, PB B.

History

Over 25 years of playing together

A history built on goodwill, community, and a lot of play.

1998

Endless Saturdays

Lekotek, a Swedish NGO, in cooperation with local youth from the Santa Ana neighborhood, launched a popular recreation space known as Endless Saturdays.

2001

Independence

Sábados Sin Fin became independent from Lekotek and was placed entirely in the hands of local volunteers—both for organizing activities and sustaining the project.

2005

A Structural Change

The project began offering specialized workshops—visual arts, photography, cinema, and theater—conducted in small groups on different days of the week.

2006

Our Own Space

A permanent headquarters was established, and new workshops were added: singing for adults, crafts for teenagers, a toy library for toddlers, and community cinema.

2010

Non-Profit Organization

Formal registration as a Civil Association before the DPPJ (Resolution No. 0877, 02/26/2010), under registration number 35,151.

Hoy

We Keep Playing

More than 25 years later, Juegoteca Sin Fin remains a 100% volunteer-run, community-driven, and entirely free space for the neighborhood.
